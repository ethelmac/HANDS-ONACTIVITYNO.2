About the Event: The Digital Shift

The **College of Computer and Information Sciences (CCIS)** proudly presents its annual week-long celebration. This year, our theme focuses on ***The Digital Shift*** in **AI, Cybersecurity, and Web Development**.

Join us for a series of **seminars**, **coding workshops**, and **competitive events**. We aim to foster a collaborative environment where students can network with industry professionals and showcase their talents.

Registration & Tech Commands

Registration URL Example

The official registration URL with URL-encoded parameters is:

https://myschool.edu/events/register?event=SMCC-CCIS%20Week%202025§ion=A

The sequence %20 in the URL stands for the *space* character.
